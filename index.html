Setup

Playback

Overall octave and tempo apply to scale/chord playback and individual note clicks; sustain is how long an individual note click rings. Separate from the drone's own settings below.

Drone

A single looped recording, pitch-shifted to the root — no custom envelope/oscillator controls below apply, and root changes retune instantly rather than gliding. Harmonium's sustain is steadier and loops more seamlessly than organ's (organ has its own built-in tremolo, which can make the loop point faintly audible).

Exact-octave layers, not detuned — they thicken the drone without adding the pulsing that Voices > 1 causes.

Voices > 1 stacks detuned copies of the oscillator — that's what causes pulsing/beating. Leave voices at 1 for a perfectly static tone.

Edge is always a single plain sawtooth (never detuned) layered underneath for grit — it won't add pulsing regardless of the mix amount.
